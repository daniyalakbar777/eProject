FAQs

1. What types of paintings do you offer?
We offer a wide range of paintings, including abstract, landscape, floral, nature, figurative, and religious paintings.

Our paintings are available on various surfaces such as canvas, acrylic sheets, fabric, paper, and OHP sheets.

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment gateways.

9. Can I return or exchange a painting?
We have a return and exchange policy for damaged or incorrect paintings. Please review our return policy for more details.

10. How can I take care of my painting?
To maintain the quality of your painting, keep it away from direct sunlight, humidity, and dust. Use a soft cloth to clean the surface gently.
